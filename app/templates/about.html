About SkillSwap

SkillSwap is a collaborative platform where users can share their knowledge and learn new skills from others.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a community where people can connect, share their expertise, and grow together. Whether you're a professional looking to teach or a learner eager to explore new skills, SkillSwap is the place for you.

How It Works

  • Browse available skills and find something you'd like to learn.
  • Offer your own skills to teach others.
  • Send and manage learning requests.
  • Communicate with other users and leave reviews.
